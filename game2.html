GAME #2

For this project I want you to create an alternate reality game.

Or, at least, an alternate reality.

I'd prefer that it be a Utopian reality, that's just me, I'm an optimist at heart.

In other words, design your ideal place/space/world....and engage us, pull us into it, lure us along into your little fantasy.

As ever, the "game" aspect of this can be as involving (game-like) as you like.

In other words, if it wants to be more story, or film, or painting or installation than it is "game", then make it so!

A variety of projects which seem to me to be in one way or another very reasonable responses to this assignment:

  • Ilya Kabokov, "The Man Who Flew Intop Space From His Apartment
  • Star Choir by Malik Gaines and Alexandro Segade
  • Marble Hornets
  • Hive Propolis
  • Tomas Saraceno: Cloud Cities
  • A.G. Rizzoli
